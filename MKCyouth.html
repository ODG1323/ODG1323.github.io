Mettu MKC Youth

MKC Youth, a vibrant community dedicated to nurturing the spiritual growth of our young members and making a positive impact in our town. At MKC Youth, we are driven by the belief in the transformative power of faith and service, inspired by the teachings of the Bible. Our mission is rooted in guiding our on their spiritual journey and encouraging them serve others with love and compassion. One of the foundational verses that shape our at MKC Youth is found in the book of Matthew, chapter 25, verses 35-36. This passage emphasizes the importance of serving those in need and showing kindness and compassion to others, reflecting the core values of our organization. We are committed to actively engaging with our community through various initiatives such as food drives, volunteering at local shelters, and providing support to those facing challenges. By following the commandment to love our neighbors as ourselves, as stated in Mark 12:31, we strive to make a tangible difference in the lives of those around us.
